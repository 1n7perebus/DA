ABOUT ME
I’m a 34-year-old from the United States with a unique background that bridges both technology and psychology. My journey began in the world of design, earning a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Industrial Design, where I specialized in Automotive Design. However, I soon found myself drawn to technology, becoming a full-stack software developer proficient in Python, JavaScript, Solidity, and a range of other technologies. I’ve built mobile apps, web applications, and even dabbled in Web3 and blockchain.

But while coding fulfills my technical curiosity, it was a deeper fascination with the human mind that truly shaped my path.

My personality type is INTP—naturally curious and analytical—qualities that led me to an unexpected but life-changing interest in psychology. It all started with an elective course in university that introduced me to philosophical and psychological thinkers like Kant, Descartes, Nietzsche, and Freud. However, it was Carl Jung’s work that truly resonated with me. His theories on the unconscious, archetypes, and personality types struck a chord, giving me language for things I had always sensed but couldn’t quite articulate.

Over the past eight years, I’ve immersed myself in Jungian psychology, exploring concepts like the Ego, Shadow, Anima/Animus, and the Collective Unconscious. Jung’s work on dream analysis, in particular, became a cornerstone of my own personal development. As I began interpreting my own dreams, I uncovered insights that shifted my perspective on myself and the human psyche in profound ways.

My dedication to psychology is deeply personal. At 13, I lost my father to suicide—a traumatic event that spurred existential crises and a determination to understand the complexities of mental health. Through my research, Jung’s work provided not only clarity but also a pathway toward healing and personal growth. This process of self-discovery has empowered me to help others in their own journeys.

Today, I use my knowledge of Jungian psychology to help individuals around the world explore their inner selves. Whether through dream analysis or one-on-one counseling, I guide people to uncover the deeper meanings in their lives, providing insights that lead to growth, healing, and greater self-awareness.

Though my journey started in the world of design and technology, it has evolved into something far more meaningful. My passion is now centered on helping others navigate their own inner worlds, using the universal language of dreams and the transformative power of psychological insight.
